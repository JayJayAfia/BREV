# Brev

**Motto:** The Minimalist Productivity Companion  
**Created by:** Jeremiah Japhet (Slack ID: Jay)

## What is it?
Brev is a minimalist productivity companion designed for professionals who want structure without clutter. It combines task management, journaling, and focus tracking into one clean, distraction-free experience.

## Why it matters
Many productivity apps overwhelm users with too many features. Brev solves this by focusing on clarity, helping users prioritize, plan, and perform better through simplicity. Itâ€™s designed to reduce distraction and mental clutter, making productivity intuitive.

## Who is it for?
- Professionals and students who feel overwhelmed by multiple productivity tools.  
- Anyone seeking a single, clean space to plan, reflect, and track progress.

## Key Features
- **Daily Planner:** Quickly organize tasks for the day.  
- **Reflection Journal:** Capture thoughts, notes, and reflections.  
- **Focus Tracker:** Visualize time spent on deep work.  
- **Analytics Overview:** Track productivity trends over time.  

## Technologies Used
- **Frontend:** Flutter or React Native for cross-platform access  
- **Backend:** Firebase for cloud sync and real-time updates  
- **Database:** Firebase Firestore for lightweight data storage
